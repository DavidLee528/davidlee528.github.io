### Overview

I am passionate about mountain bike enduro, trail and road running, badminton, photography, and reading. For over a decade, I have pursued [Mountain Bike Enduro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enduro_(mountain_biking)) with my [Santa Cruz Megatower CC](https://www.santacruzbicycles.com/en-EU/bikes/megatower), sharing my adventures on [Bilibili](https://space.bilibili.com/288683260). Off the bike, I enjoy [trail running](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trail_running) and playing [badminton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Badminton) indoors. As a wildlife photographer, I capture striking images with my [Canon EOS/EF](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canon_EF_camera) system. Additionally, I value reading as a way to continuously learn and grow.
